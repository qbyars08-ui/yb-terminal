LPTH — LightPath Technologies

Thesis

LightPath makes infrared optical components — freeform optics, molded glass lenses, IR imaging optics — for defense, industrial imaging, and photonics supply chain customers. The company acquired Visimid Technologies (IR thermal imaging modules) and is pivoting revenue mix from legacy commercial (phones, cameras, drift-risk) to defense + industrial imaging, which carry higher margins and longer contracts.

The Frontier-sleeve bet: as the photonics supercycle rolls through data centers, the same fabs and optical suppliers that serve datacom also serve defense IR, thermal imaging, and directed-energy adjacents. LPTH is a small-cap, specialty-optics pure play sitting in that adjacency. Not a core position. Small sizing. Asymmetric upside if defense revenue compounds; cut if commercial drag resumes.
